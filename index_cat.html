  • My Cat

I am a thirteenth year Research Assistant in cat-onomics working with Ward Reesman. I am interested in research at the intersection of petting and napping, with a particular focus in belly rubs, pieces of string, sun basking, and food. Through my training with Ward, I have developed specialized skills in opening doors and burrowing under blankets. My current work focuses on the development of a robust apartment escape methodology.
