I am an International Economist in the Research Division of the Office of Economics at the U.S. International Trade Commission. I received my Ph.D. in Economics from Clemson University in 2024.

My research interests are in international trade, applied econometrics, and industrial organization. My current work focuses on the quantitative evaluation of trade policy.
