I am a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at the John E. Walker Department of Economics at Clemson University. My research interests are in international trade with a focus on trade policy, applied econometrics, and industrial organization. My current research focuses on the trade effects of temporary trade barriers and the origination of economic integration agreements. On this website you can find my CV and summaries of my teaching and research.

I am available for interviews on the 2023-2024 job market.
